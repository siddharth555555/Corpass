Corpass — Go-To-Market & Onboarding Playbook

Interactive founder guide: from zero → first users → operational scale.

Stage Tracker

Business Order

Don't launch marketplace-first. Launch transaction-first.

1. Choose one wedge

Pick ONE category: office supplies, IT procurement, furniture, etc. Target one city and one buyer profile.

2. Onboard sellers first

Recruit 10–20 suppliers manually. Upload catalog for them. Offer white-glove onboarding.

3. Acquire buyers manually

Find 5–10 companies. Run procurement via WhatsApp + Corpass backend until patterns stabilize.

4. Force RFQ usage

Don't optimize checkout. Optimize quote → order conversion.

5. Build trust loops

Payment ledger → invoice → asset auto-log → review.

30 Day Execution

  1. Week 1: 15 suppliers
  2. Week 2: 5 buyers
  3. Week 3: 20 completed RFQs
  4. Week 4: First repeat buyer

North Star Metrics

  • RFQ→Order %
  • Repeat Buyers
  • Seller Activation
  • GMV
  • Asset Logging Rate
