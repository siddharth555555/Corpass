- **Title**: B2B Procurement Is Broken at Every Step
- **Content**:
  - Buyers send RFQs via WhatsApp and email — no tracking, no audit trail
  - Order tracking lives in spreadsheets — status is a phone call away
  - Payment reconciliation is manual — "Did you pay?" / "I didn't receive it"
  - Procured assets disappear into corners — no one knows what the company owns
  - Vendor reliability is word-of-mouth — no verified transaction history
  - Result: 30%+ of procurement time is spent on coordination, not purchasing

## Page 12 [content]
- **Title**: The Order Lifecycle: Transparent, Negotiable, Accountable
- **Content**:
  - PLACED: Buyer places order or sends inquiry
  - COUNTER_OFFERED: Buyer or seller proposes new price/qty — negotiate until both agree
  - CONFIRMED: Seller accepts. Order is locked.
  - SHIPPED: Stock auto-deducts. Buyer gets notified.
  - DELIVERED: Invoice auto-generates. Asset auto-logs to buyer dashboard.
  - PAYMENT: Buyer records payment with UTR. Seller acknowledges or disputes.
  - REVIEW: Both parties rate each other. Verified. Real.
